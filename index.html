About me

My name is Illai.

I am an entrepreneur and a design and product expert. I am passionate about building stuff, startups, marketing, growth and street fitness.

I was born raised in Tel Aviv, Israel, but traveled a lot, lived and worked in places like Brazil and Turkey.

I started my career as Patent Engineer in a medical startup
called Medingo, and grew up in the company to become an Innovation Envengalist after the company was acquired by Roche Diagnostics. Today I am the founder and CEO of Mobifile, and I'm also consulting at a robotics startup XACT Robotics, and an Advisor at cRoundCam.

I am contributer at Linkedin and Medium and also running a Facebook group called Startup Articles, where anyone can read and share articles and blog posts about startups.
